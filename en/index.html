HIRAETH

choreograpy
Carolina Márquez
music
Carmen G. Jara

general public
55 minutes
premiered November 2022
Teatro Echegaray, Málaga
synopsis
Hiraeth is the search for a place where technology, nature and humanity reside in harmony.

It is precisely this search that begins a common creative process, from sound research to movement, from corporeality to the score. Starting from abstraction, this artistic project raises important topics such as the experience with our natural environment, extinction, the imbalances that technology can generate in our lives and the urgent need to recover empathy and love as a form of connection between human beings. Through a poetic visual language, the work invites the public to be present, to reflect on these questions. Is it possible to restart?

INCISIÓN

choreograpy
Carolina Márquez
music
Carmen G. Jara
*
general public
25 minutes
premiered April 2023
Centre Pompidou Málaga
synopsis
Choreography inspired by the work of the plastic artist Lucio Fontana.

One of his most distinctive elements are the incisions he makes on the canvas. It traverses the empty space, creating a real indentation. Fontana transforms the surface into a new spatial state, leaving a hole that defies the viewer's imagination. This obsession with traversing that the artist proposes is the guiding thread of this performance, where the performers emulate the artist's gesture and go beyond the space with their movement.
