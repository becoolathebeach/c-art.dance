Carolina Marquez Rivas

Carolina Márquez is a dancer, choreographer, and educator specializing in Graham technique, with training from the Martha Graham School in New York, Trinity Laban in London, and the Superior Conservatory of Dance in Málaga. She has taught at institutions in the U.S. and Spain, including New York Theater Ballet and the Málaga Conservatory. She has organized workshops with former Martha Graham principal dancers and led the first Graham Teacher Workshop in Spain. As a performer and choreographer, she has contributed to various international festivals and was co-director of caraBdanza. Currently, she directs C-Art Dance and represents “Graham for Europe” in Spain.
