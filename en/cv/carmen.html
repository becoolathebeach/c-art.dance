Carmen G. Jara

Carmen García Jara is a Spanish pianist, composer, and educator. Her formational studies were at the Conservatory of Music in Alicante and in 2012 she obtained a PhD from the University of Málaga. She has expanded her training with Rex Lobo, Solomon Mikowsky, John Salmon and Doru Pop amongst others. Carmen has performed internationally with chamber ensembles and has collaborated with major dance institutions like the Spanish National Ballet and Dutch National Opera and Ballet. Her musical compositions span theatre, film, and dance, including multi-award winners Una mujer Feliz (2019) and Hiraeth (2022). In 2022 she co-founded (and continues to direct) C-Art.dance in addition to teaching and research studies at the Conservatory of Dance in Malaga.
