Carmen G. Jara

Carmen García Jara is a Spanish pianist, composer, and educator with a doctorate in music. She has performed internationally with chamber ensembles and collaborated with major dance institutions like the Spanish National Ballet. Her compositions span theatre, film, and dance, including El sueño de Degás (2015) and Hiraeth (2022), earning multiple awards. She co-founded C-Art Dance with choreographer Carolina Márquez and continues to teach and research in Malaga.
